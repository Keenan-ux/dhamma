# Draft outreach email (BCBS / Access to Insight)

**To:** contact@buddhistinquiry.org

**Subject:** A mirror of the Access to Insight library at dhamma.fly.dev

Dear Barre Center for Buddhist Studies and the Access to Insight maintainers,

My name is [name]. I am writing to introduce myself and to let you
know, plainly, what I have done with the Access to Insight library.

I keep a small scholarly tool for reading and searching the Pali canon
and its commentaries across traditions, at https://dhamma.fly.dev. It
is non-commercial, with no advertising and no tracking. It exists
mostly so I and a few others can read these texts and their
translations side by side.

I have downloaded the Access to Insight offline edition and preserved
its library within the tool, under the CC BY-NC 4.0 terms it is
offered on. Every translation and article carries its translator's
name and an attribution back to Access to Insight, and the
non-canonical material sits in its own Library section rather than
mixed in with the canonical texts. I took some care to keep each
attribution intact.

I know Access to Insight has been winding down, and I wanted you to
hear directly from me that the library is mirrored and being looked
after, rather than to discover it on your own.

If anything about the attribution should read differently, or if there
is material you would prefer I take down, please tell me and I will
make the change. I am happy to defer to your wishes on any of it.

With respect and gratitude for the work you have done,
