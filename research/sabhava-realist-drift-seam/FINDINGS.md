# Sabhava realist-collocate density at the canon-to-commentary register seam

## Abstract

H1 (the house register thesis, after Ronkin) predicted that realist own-nature vocabulary crystallizes as a packaged definitional apparatus at the canon-to-commentary boundary: specifically, a step-change UP in sabhava density, in paramattha, and above all in the co-occurrence of sabhava with paramattha and the definitional tetrad, across the 4para (para-canonical / mula) to 5comm (atthakatha) work-role boundary, with the boundary as the largest jump.

The structural half of the prediction holds. The full four-term tetrad (the lakkhanadi-catukka) is exactly absent (0 per Mchar) in every canon stratum (1early, 2late, 3abh, 4para) and appears only from 5comm (0.293/Mchar) and 6tika (0.494/Mchar). The packaged definitional frame is a commentary and sub-commentary construction, not a canon feature.

The quantitative half fails. The primary headline measure, realist co-occurrence (sabhava plus paramattha plus at least one tetrad term), moves the wrong way at the named boundary: 2.474/Mchar in 4para falls to 0.391/Mchar in 5comm, a ratio of 0.16x, a roughly sixfold DROP exactly where H1 predicts a rise. Both flagship realist signals peak at 6tika, not 5comm. The negative controls confirm rather than rescue the signal: the raw sabhava rise that does exist sits inside the generic commentary-verbosity envelope, and the realist-specific co-occurrence declined while the procedural control sat flat-to-down, so there is no realist-specific positive seam at the named boundary to control-subtract.

Two pre-registered safeguards also fired against the finding. Falsifier 4 tripped: padatthana, predicted near-absent in 4para mula, is already at 11.544/Mchar in 4para, essentially equal to 5comm (11.795/Mchar, ratio 1.02x). And the mandatory blind sense audit (Q8) never ran (the coding table did not exist), so p_realist, the inter-annotator agreement, and the realist-sense-weighted sabhava curve that the method names as the headline statistic do not exist. Per the protocol's own agreement gate, only the raw masked stem density is available and it is reported flagged inconclusive.

Net: report a null on H1's specific 4para to 5comm realist crystallization. What is supported is the weaker, control-independent structural claim that the packaged catukka frame is absent from canon and present only from commentary onward, a corpus corroboration of an already published register observation rather than a new quantitative result.

## The question and why it is a fair test

The protocol pre-registered H1 against a pre-committed null (H0) and froze the lexica, the direction, and the falsifiers before the serial run. The design is a fair test for four reasons.

First, every magnitude is per-character (per Mchar over is_primary rows), so a commentary stratum being simply longer or wordier cannot by itself manufacture a jump.

Second, the primary statistic is a co-occurrence within a passage (sabhava with paramattha and at least one tetrad term), not a single polysemous stem, which targets the packaged apparatus rather than any one loose word.

Third, the design carries two negative controls. The primary procedural control is udayabbaya (the arising-and-passing-away insight-stage label), a genuinely commentarial path-technical term that should be present and even denser in commentary but must NOT show the sharp realist-specific boundary jump. The secondary control is anicca, an all-layer doctrinal term used to read off the generic commentary-verbosity baseline. The protocol states the discriminating quantity is the realist jump MINUS the udayabbaya jump, and that if the control jumps as hard as sabhava the realist signal is a generic-register artifact.

Fourth, the design pre-committed a positive control and a falsifier on the tetrad discriminators themselves: padatthana and paccupatthana are near-exclusive to the commentary catukka frame, so their near-absence in 4para mula was set as a confirmation that the apparatus is a commentary construction, with their presence in mula set as a falsifier of that framing.

One caveat on the test frame that must travel with every number: stratum is a work_slug lookup, a textual-role label, not dated chronology. A boundary here is a canon-versus-commentary register seam, never proof of diachronic semantic change in real time.

## What the canon shows

Across the canon strata the realist apparatus is sparse-to-absent. The full four-term tetrad (lakkhana and rasa and paccupatthana and padatthana together, the lakkhanadi-catukka proper) is 0 per Mchar in 1early, 2late, 3abh, and 4para. paramattha is near-absent in the early canon (0.611/Mchar in 1early, 2.376 in 3abh) and rises only into the para-canonical and commentary strata. The realist co-occurrence is 0 in 1early and 3abh.

The one canon stratum that misbehaves is 4para. It is a thin stratum: 294 primary rows and 1.213 Mchar, against 30.691 Mchar for 5comm and 28.358 Mchar for 6tika. On that thin base, padatthana is already at 11.544/Mchar, essentially equal to 5comm (11.795/Mchar). That trips pre-registered falsifier 4: a tetrad discriminator the protocol predicted near-absent in 4para mula is already dense there. The reason is locus-specific: 4para is the para-canonical Nettippakarana and Petakopadesa (work_slugs pli-ne, pli-pe) plus Milindapanha (pli-mil), texts that already carry the proto-apparatus. Per-Mchar densities on a 1.213-Mchar stratum are single-passage-sensitive and must not be stated with stable-stratum confidence; Q12 shows the 4para realist signal reduces to a single full-package hit in pli-pe over 0.294 Mchar.

## What the commentary does, and the falsifiable core

H1's falsifiable core is the realist co-occurrence step at 4para to 5comm. The executed data contradicts it.

| Measure (per Mchar) | 4para | 5comm | 6tika | 4para to 5comm |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Realist co-occurrence (Q5) | 2.474 (3 hits) | 0.391 (12 hits) | 2.363 (67 hits) | 0.16x (a ~6x DROP) |
| Full four-term tetrad (Q6) | 0.000 | 0.293 (9 hits) | 0.494 (14 hits) | 0 to nonzero |
| Sabhava raw masked (Q2) | 23.912 | 67.251 | 165.881 | 2.81x |
| Paramattha (Q3) | 9.070 | 16.878 | 27.647 | 1.86x |
| Padatthana (Q4) | 11.544 | 11.795 | 20.206 | 1.02x (flat) |
| Paccupatthana (Q4) | 1.649 | 10.035 | 12.942 | 6.09x |

Three things follow. The headline realist co-occurrence drops roughly sixfold across the exact boundary where H1 says it should jump. The structural tetrad frame is genuinely a commentary phenomenon (0 in all canon strata, first nonzero at 5comm), which is the control-independent positive that survives. And both flagship realist signals peak at 6tika, not 5comm: sabhava raw 165.881/Mchar, paramattha 27.647/Mchar, realist co-occurrence 2.363/Mchar at 6tika (6.04x above 5comm). The realist co-occurrence package therefore looks closer to concentrating at the sub-commentary than to crystallizing at the commentary boundary; the apparatus is more a 6tika phenomenon than a 5comm one. The protocol's secondary hypothesis (intensification from 5comm to 6tika rather than origination) is mixed: raw sabhava and paramattha intensify into 6tika consistent with the secondary prediction, but the realist co-occurrence package rises 6.04x and the full tetrad rises 1.68x into 6tika, which reads closer to origination at the sub-commentary than to mere intensification of an already-present 5comm apparatus.

A work-dominance guard undercuts the already-weak 5comm signal further. Of the 12 5comm realist co-occurrence hits, 9 (75 percent) are pli-kn-attha; pli-vism, the protocol's named exemplar locus for the catukka, contributes 0 realist co-occurrence hits in 5comm (Q12). The 6tika signal is far more distributed (pli-abh-tika 19, pli-dn-tika 14, pli-mn-tika 8 of 67). The 5comm realist density is largely a single-text artifact.

The headline statistic the method actually names, realist-sense-weighted sabhava density, does not exist. Q8 errored (the sabhava_codes table did not exist), so the blind sense audit was never coded, no inter-annotator agreement was computed, and there is no p_realist per stratum. Per the protocol's own agreement gate, the fallback is to report the raw masked stem density flagged inconclusive and to NOT assert a realist curve. All sabhava-sense statements here are therefore polysemy-unresolved raw stem, not own-nature counts.

## The negative-control result

The controls refute rather than rescue the signal.

The primary procedural control udayabbaya did NOT jump at 4para to 5comm: 9.070/Mchar to 6.288/Mchar, a ratio of 0.69x, a slight decline. In isolation that behaves as the protocol wanted (a procedural term that does not crystallize). But the headline realist co-occurrence ALSO declined across that boundary (0.16x), so the realist signal failed in the same downward direction the control sat flat-to-down. There is no realist-specific positive seam to subtract a control from. The control-subtracted realist co-occurrence jump at the boundary is about +0.70/Mchar, indistinguishable from noise and attributable to the control's own negative drift rather than to a realist-specific rise.

The secondary control anicca rose 2.09x at 4para to 5comm (22.263/Mchar to 46.593/Mchar). The raw masked sabhava rise (2.81x) sits inside that same generic-verbosity envelope. So the one sabhava boundary rise that does exist is not separable from generic commentary register that anicca also shows.

The only control-independent positive is the structural absence-then-presence of the full four-term tetrad: 0/Mchar in every canon stratum, nonzero only from 5comm (0.293) and 6tika (0.494). Neither control replicates that pattern, so that register-distribution observation stands.

## What it means and what it does NOT

It means: the packaged definitional tetrad (the lakkhanadi-catukka) is a commentary and sub-commentary register feature, absent from the canon strata measured here and present only from atthakatha onward. That is a corpus corroboration of a register pattern already published in the secondary literature, not a discovery of this study.

It does NOT mean H1 is confirmed. The novel quantitative claim H1 owns, a realist co-occurrence step UP at 4para to 5comm with that boundary as the largest jump, is falsified on the executed data: the measure dropped roughly sixfold at the boundary and peaks at the sub-commentary instead. Falsifier 4 also tripped (padatthana already dense in 4para). The verdict is mixed leaning null: structural origination holds, the named quantitative boundary step does not.

Scope limits that bound every statement above:
- This is a register and distribution claim only. It cannot adjudicate whether Theravada commentators held a metaphysically realist view of dhammas. That doctrinal debate is out of scope and no realism verdict is offered.
- stratum is a work_slug textual-role lookup, not dated chronology. Boundary means a canon-versus-commentary register seam, never diachronic semantic change.
- A stratum-versus-layer disagreement is true by construction (layer is derived from work_slug), so it is never cited as independent evidence.
- Co-occurrence within a passage is a proximity proxy for the apparatus, not a parse of definitional syntax.
- The sense audit never executed, so no realist-sense-weighted curve exists; the masked stem is reported inconclusive.
- The masked stem is not a clean own-nature count: the broad sabh[aa]v stem catches at least one bhava-compound not in the exclusion mask (tidasabhavanam, tidasa+bhavana, the Tavatimsa realm; id mil5.1.4).
- 4para is a single-passage-sensitive stratum (1.213 Mchar, 3 raw realist hits; Q12 reduces the full package to 1 hit in pli-pe), so any 4para ratio is unstable in magnitude and sign.
- The 5comm realist signal is work-dominated (9 of 12 hits pli-kn-attha; pli-vism contributes 0).

## Denominator note and auditability

A denominator drift must be recorded. The protocol froze the per-role denominators (mula 53.543921 Mchar, attha 29.488533, tika 28.357746) and Q0 was the sanity check. The live Q0 returns mula = 26.939812 Mchar, roughly half the frozen value (attha 29.488533 and tika 28.357746 match). The corpus the protocol was written against is not exactly the corpus that ran on the mula side. The per-stratum densities here were computed on the live Q1 denominators (4para 1.212772 Mchar, 5comm 30.691214, 6tika 28.357746), not the frozen ones, so the frozen-denominator sanity check is treated as failed and the ratios are read off the live bases.

Every count in this finding re-derives from _protocol.json and _raw.json. Q1 supplies the per-stratum character denominators. Q2 through Q6 supply the per-Mchar densities and co-occurrence counts. Q9 and Q10 supply the two controls. Q12 supplies the per-work breakout behind the work-dominance guard. Q7 holds the 265-row sense-audit sample (263 of 265 with null translation fields, so coders would code from Pali); Q8 holds the error that confirms the audit never ran. The headline ratios recompute exactly: realist co-occurrence 5comm/4para = 0.391/2.474 = 0.158; 6tika/5comm = 2.363/0.391 = 6.04; raw sabhava 5comm/4para = 67.251/23.912 = 2.81; udayabbaya 5comm/4para = 6.288/9.070 = 0.69; anicca 5comm/4para = 46.593/22.263 = 2.09; padatthana 5comm/4para = 11.795/11.544 = 1.02.
