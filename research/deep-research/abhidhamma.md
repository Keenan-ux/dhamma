# Suttas → Abhidhamma → commentary: systematization vs "hardening"

*Deep-research report (DR-5), 2026-06-25. Workflow: 102 agents, ~48 min, 5 search angles → 20 sources →
70 claims → 25 adversarially verified (24 confirmed / 1 killed).*

## Headline
The **systematization** half of the thesis is scholarly consensus: canon→Abhidhamma→commentary is a
progressive formalization of techniques/terms the suttas leave open. The **"hardening into metaphysics"**
half is genuinely contested, not settled — and verification surfaced one tension that bears directly on our
sabhāva prereg (below).

## (a) Position map
- **Origins — Frauwallner / Gethin (continuity, not rupture).** Gethin rejects the simple "mātikā = early
  name for the Abhidhamma" equation. The Abhidhamma evolves out of an existing practice of taking a list and
  expanding it, going back to the beginnings of Buddhist literature. mātikā (from mātṛ, "mother"; cognate
  with *matrix*) is a **generative seed, not a summary**. Vibhaṅga + Puggalapaññatti are structured as
  commentaries on such lists. Verified verbatim from the downloaded PDF (Gethin 1992; SEP Abhidharma). 3–0.
- **Momentariness (khaṇavāda) — post-canonical (von Rospatt, Kim, SEP).** "Cannot be traced back to the four
  Pāli Nikāyas… initially a theory created by Buddhist scholastic tradition." The 16/17-thought-moment matter
  duration + three sub-moments (uppāda/ṭhiti/bhaṅga) develop "first in the aṭṭhakathās and then in the
  ṭīkās." The Vibhaṅga-aṭṭhakathā itself says momentariness is "confined to the Abhidhamma, not found in the
  Suttas." 3–0 on dating + aṭṭhakathā→ṭīkā trajectory; 2–1 on the Vibh-a citation (verified verbatim).
- **Two truths (sammuti/paramattha) — Karunadasa.** "A doctrinal innovation of the Abhidhamma" with Nikāya
  nītattha/neyyattha seeds, denoting **"two ways of presenting what is true" rather than two grades** — "one
  kind of truth is not held superior to the other"; "the person exists" is "not erroneous." Converges with
  Heim; cuts AGAINST reading two-truths as metaphysical hardening, while dating the formal scheme to Abhidhamma. 3–0.
- **Counter-position — Heim & Ram-Prasad (the realist reading is contested even at the Buddhaghosa layer).**
  The Theravāda Abhidhamma is a method-giving, phenomenological project, NOT a Sarvāstivāda-style ontology.
  Ronkin's case rests on question-begging translations ("self-existents," "individual essence" — the latter
  Ñāṇamoli gave "with significant misgivings"). Falsifier: at Vism XIV.127 Buddhaghosa subdivides the
  sabhāva of feeling into one/three/five — incompatible with sabhāva naming a final irreducible existent.
  3–0. (Amod Lele argues the opposite — Buddhaghosa DOES rank the ultimate ontologically — so this is a live
  dispute, not a settled refutation of Ronkin.)

## (b) Asserted-but-never-counted claims — status
- **Ronkin — realist sabhāva drift localized "in the Aṭṭhakathā, not the ṭīkā"** ⚠️ **Contradicted.** See the
  flagged tension below.
- **Hamilton — khandha×āyatana×dhātu cross-classification + rūpakkhandha-classification of sense faculties
  "only in the Abhidhamma"** — NOT verified against Hamilton's own text (archive.org copy unreliable). Gethin
  corroborates the general structural direction but is not Hamilton.
- **PED — abhiññā narrowed to fixed six (chaḷabhiññā) "nine times out of ten" in commentaries** — the
  "9-in-10" WORDING was not verified. The Encyclopedia of Indian Religions treats abhiññā as the closed
  six-fold list (canonical looser/three-fold sense it narrowed from). The systematized sense is real; the PED
  quantification stands uncounted.

**THE FLAGGED TENSION (medium-confidence, operator-actionable):** the brief's premise that **Ronkin
localizes the realist crystallization in the aṭṭhakathā, not the ṭīkā** is contradicted by secondary reports
of Ronkin's actual position. Wikipedia (citing *Early Buddhist Metaphysics* p.112) and SEP put the
ontological-realist shift in the **later ṭīkā**, with the aṭṭhakathā still "pragmatic and psychological"
(sabhāva there used "for the sake of determining the dhammas' individuality, not their existential status").
**Our local prereg inherited the same aṭṭhakathā framing — and our own corpus data lands on the ṭīkā side**
(realist package peaks at 6tīkā 2.363/Mchar, not 5comm/aṭṭhakathā 0.391). Not adjudicated against Ronkin's
2005 primary text; should be before publishing any "Ronkin says aṭṭhakathā" claim. 2–1.

One claim **killed (0–3):** a purported Routledge chapter "The Development of the Concept of Sabhava…" —
verifiers could not confirm it exists; excluded.

## (d) Key Pāli terms + loci
- **mātikā** — generative matrix, not summary; localized to Dhammasaṅgaṇī / Vibhaṅga (Gethin 1992 p.164).
  (Gethin's two axes are triplet-couplet × core; khandha×āyatana×dhātu sits inside the core mātikā.)
- **sabhāva** — absent from suttas; **absent from the Abhidhamma Piṭaka books themselves**; barely in
  para-canon; standard only post-canonically (Vism VIII 246; Dhs-sub-commentary). Corpus-verified: 0 genuine
  hits in the 7 canonical Abhidhamma books (5 apparent = purisabhāvo "maleness"); Netti 2, Peṭakopadesa 2,
  Paṭis 1, then **aṭṭhakathā 533, ṭīkā 1,551.**
- **paramattha / sammuti** — Abhidhamma innovation, Nikāya seeds (Karunadasa).
- **abhiññā** — closed six-fold chaḷabhiññā in commentarial usage (EIR); canonical sense looser/three-fold.
- **lakkhaṇa-rasa-paccupaṭṭhāna(-padaṭṭhāna) tetrad** — 0 in canon, appears only from 5comm onward.

## (e) What density + read-back can and cannot settle
CAN: demonstrate a **register distribution** — a term/apparatus absent in canon, present only from commentary
onward (strongest positive; e.g. the tetrad's clean 0→present step).
CANNOT by raw per-character density alone: distinguish a genuine trend from fluctuation without a formal test
(Hilpert & Gries 2009); avoid a difference-over-similarity bias (a harness flagging only where commentary is
denser over-reports "hardening" — must also test continuity, Gabrielatos 2018); claim objectivity; treat the
6 stratum boundaries as data-derived (they are analyst-imposed); assign a fixed valence to one count metric;
**adjudicate whether commentators held a realist metaphysics** (the Heim-vs-Ronkin question, out of reach of
any count). METHOD CAVEAT: in our corpus "stratum" is a work_slug textual-role lookup, NOT dated chronology —
every boundary is a canon-vs-commentary register seam. The masked sabhāva stem is polysemy-unresolved, so
raw stem density is NOT an own-nature count.
