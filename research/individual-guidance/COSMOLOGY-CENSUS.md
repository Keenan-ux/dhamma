# The world-description arm: is the open→literal hardening multi-jump, and how much is real?

*A gated, sense-audited stratum-distribution census of the cosmology / physical-claims domain, built to
the persons-domain bar (`HARDENING-CENSUS.md`). Pre-registered in `COSMOLOGY-PREREG.md` (frozen before
the run); scored verbatim below. Built 2026-06-22 against the live corpus (194,710 rows). Method: the
fine 6-stratum count harness (`run_cosmology.py`, one serial connection), truncated stems (a 0 is robust
only on the truncated root), the technical compound not the bare simplex, and a row-level sense audit of
every positive early count. Raw counts: `COSMOLOGY-COUNTS.json`. Enumeration: `COSMOLOGY-ENUMERATION.json`.*

## Hypotheses (from the prereg)

- **H1:** the open→literal concretization recurs across the world-description domain (figurative / open /
  hyperbolic / measureless early → bounded / measured / named / reified late), jump-localized.
- **H0:** single-jump only; OR counters dominate; OR the gradient is wholly definitional.

## The fine stratum code (work_slug → stratum)

1early (four Nikāyas + early Khuddaka) · 2late (late Khuddaka) · 3abh (Abhidhamma) · 4para
(Netti/Peṭaka/Milinda) · 5comm (commentary + Visuddhimagga) · 6tika (sub-commentary). A 7th bucket,
7other, holds the extra-canonical *anya* works; it is reported in the raw counts but does not carry the
verdict. (The `pli-kn` Khuddaka-catch-all sits in 2late here; ITEM 2 re-buckets it. Its effect on this
census is bounded: every compound support is 0 in *both* 1early and 2late, so re-bucketing pli-kn cannot
move a zero-vs-present headline.)

## Sense audit (every positive early count read on sampled rows)

The bare simplex over-counts the target sense; the technical compound is the clean discriminator (P5).
Read rows (`_COSMOLOGY-SAMPLES.json`):

- **avīci.** Bare *avīc* = 284 early rows, **zero** of them the hell: they are *avitakka-avicāra* /
  *vicāra* / *vicintaka* / *yāva icchanti*. The named *avīcimahāniraya* (four-gated, heated-iron) is 0 in
  the four Nikāyas. Homograph guard load-bearing.
- **ussada.** Bare *ussada* = 119 early, **zero** the hell: *candan-ussada* (sandal-anointed), the
  substring inside *man-ussa-dhamma*, and *ussada* "prominences" (*yassussadā natthi*). The
  *ussada-niraya* apparatus is 0 canon.
- **anubyañjana.** Bare *anubyañjana* = 75 early, the sense-restraint idiom (*suttaso anubyañjanaso*; *na
  nimittaggāhī nānubyañjanaggāhī*), not the marks. The *asīti-anubyañjana* set is 0 in the four Nikāyas.
- **the Eight Hells' names.** *sañjīva* early = the disciple-name Vidhura-Sañjīva (MN 50, DN 14);
  *kālasutta* early = sutta-titles (*Kāla-sutta*, on Times); *patāpana* late = the cakkavatti king
  Mahāpatāpa. The closed-set term *aṭṭhamahāniraya* is commentarial; the enumerating locus is the late
  Jātaka (ja530 / kn14).
- **avyākata.** The finite/infinite-world refusal is *antavā loko* (31 early). Bare *abyākata* (1,018
  Abhidhamma rows) is the ethical class "karmically-indeterminate", a different sense, correctly excluded.
- **aparimāṇa.** The bare stem is contaminated by *parimaṇḍala* (*nigrodha-parimaṇḍala*,
  *gula-parimaṇḍala*); the clean anchor is Snp 1.8 / Kp 9 Mettasutta *mānasaṃ bhāvaye aparimāṇaṃ*.
- **caturāsītiyojanasahassa.** The 2 early rows are **AN 7.66** (Sattasūriya), which gives Sineru's exact
  84,000-yojana dimensions in the Buddha's voice. This is the genuine article, not a homograph: the
  figure is early-canonical. (See the correction below.)
- **kāmāvacara.** The 2 early rows are DN 1 adjectival (*atta dibbo rūpī kāmāvacaro*, a postulated self),
  not the taxonomy term; *rūpāvacara/arūpāvacara* are 0 in the four Nikāyas. The closed -avacara classifier
  is the Abhidhamma's.
- **anotatta.** The 5 early rows are the cosmic lake itself (Thag 6.10, AN 7.66, the Vinaya alms-flight),
  but as an incidental named feature reached by psychic power, not a dimensioned installation. Coded mixed.
- **lokantarika / antarā-parinibbāyī.** The early rows carry the open form (the birth-marvel dark void
  flooded by a measureless light; the in-between non-returner), not the late concretization (the located
  hell; the reified *antarābhava*). Coded by the compound.

## Caveats and verification

- **Double-encoding inflates the canonical-stratum counts.** The corpus ingests canonical content under
  two id schemes, the SuttaCentral bilara-data ids and the CST ids, so a single early sutta is counted as
  two passage-rows (the 2 early *caturāsītiyojanasahassa* rows are one AN 7.66 verse, `an7.66` +
  `cst-s0403m3.mul-an7_3`; the 2 early *cakkavāḷa* rows are one DN 16 verse; the 2 early *mahāmeru* rows are
  one MN 50 verse). The commentary and sub-commentary strata are CST-only, with no SuttaCentral duplicate.
  The effect is one-directional: it inflates the *early* counts relative to the commentary, so the real
  canon-vs-commentary contrast is even sharper than the table shows, and the supports are strengthened, not
  weakened. No zero-vs-present headline is affected (a 0 stays 0; a 2 that is one unique verse stays present).
  The same double-encoding affects the pli-kn Khuddaka catch-all (ITEM 2).
- **Verification.** Every positive early count and every commentary-sense reading in the table was audited
  on sampled rows by the coordinator directly (`_COSMOLOGY-SAMPLES.json`), serially, against the live
  corpus. An additional adversarial agent pass was started but aborted mid-run: the verification agents
  attempted to re-measure against dhamma-pg with their own concurrent connections, which the project's
  serial-only DB rule forbids, so it was stopped and the zombie backends terminated. The agents that did
  complete confirmed the coding (the *lokantarika* and *mahāmeru/sumeru* support verdicts) and surfaced the
  double-encoding caveat above. The verdicts therefore rest on the coordinator's direct row audit, not on a
  fanned-out re-count.

## The correction the census produced (Sineru's height)

The paper's draft read "Mount Sineru is an early simile of greatness whose exact height, eighty-four
thousand *yojana*, is sub-commentarial." The census falsifies the second half. **AN 7.66 states Sineru's
84,000-yojana dimensions in the four Nikāyas** (*sineru, bhikkhave, pabbatarājā caturāsītiyojanasahassāni
āyāmena…*). The honest finding: Sineru is named as a simile of greatness early *and* given its 84,000-yojana
measure early; what is late is the **systematic** dimensioning of *every* cosmic feature in yojanas (the
gazetteer) and the bounded rim-wall that makes the sphere an object. The unit, the headline Sineru figure,
and the lifespan/era figures are all canonical; the systematization of them is what the later strata add.
This is logged as a falsified prediction-leg (P1) below.

## The count

Of 24 distinct transitions: **14 support** · **4 mixed** · **6 counter**. Among the support/mixed set,
14/18 ≈ **78% clean support**. The supports land at four distinct jumps, not one:

1. **early-canonical → Abhidhamma** — the closed three-realm *-avacara* taxonomy (0→~290); the reified
   *antarābhava* seeded in the Kathāvatthu.
2. **late-canonical → commentary** — the fathom-aura *byāmappabhā* (Buddhavaṃsa/Apadāna first); the
   per-Buddha cubit heights (the *asītihattha* Buddhavaṃsa seed); the closed Eight Hells enumerated in the
   late Jātaka ja530.
3. **canon → commentary** (the bulk) — the rim-wall, the 8,000-yojana continent, the four-continent map,
   the located void-hell, the sixteen subsidiary hells, the destruction machinery, the named cycle-phases,
   the 80 marks, the 18-cubit body.
4. **commentary → sub-commentary** — *cakkavāḷapabbata* (36→34), *kappavināsa* (27→32), *antarābhava*
   (37→133), *sīmāsambheda* (11→37), and the yojana measuring (2,530→5,430) all densify further. The
   latest layer keeps hardening, as in the persons domain.

## Verdict

H1 is **substantially supported but not universal**, and H0 is **partly true on its (b) leg only**. The
concretization is genuinely **multi-jump** (H0(a) single-jump rejected), the counters are a real but
**minority** current (six of twenty-four; H0(b) counters-dominate rejected), and the gradient is **not
wholly definitional** (H0(c) rejected): there are Buddha-voiced early-open anchors independent of the
measuring-sorts-late circularity (the DN 16 hyperbolic plural for *cakkavāḷa*; the marvel-light void;
the *antarā-parinibbāyī* agent-term; the *antavā loko*, *anamatagga*, and *appamāṇā* refusals). The
honest shape matches the persons domain: a strong multi-jump hardening trend against a backdrop of cases
the early canon already fixed (Sineru's height, lifespans, MN 127's graded extent) and cases the tradition
keeps open or refuses to close (the finite/infinite world, beginningless saṃsāra, boundless mettā). The
world arm is the thinner and freshly-counted sample, and it points the same way.
