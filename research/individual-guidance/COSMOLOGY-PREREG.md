# Pre-registration — the cosmology arm of "From Function to Essence"

*Frozen 2026-06-22, before the gated/sense-audited census is run. The persons domain already has its
gated census of record (`HARDENING-CENSUS.md`); this brings the world-description arm to the same bar.
Honest disclosure: an un-gated 47-candidate seed (`COSMOLOGY-CENSUS-RAW.json`) was measured earlier by
the discovery sweep, so this is not a blind first look at the data. What is pre-committed here, and scored
verbatim afterward, is (1) the deduplicated transition set, (2) the technical-compound stems to test, (3)
the homograph/sense-audit protocol, and (4) the decision rule. The value of the freeze is that the
verdict logic and the audit discipline are fixed before the rigorous fine-6-stratum re-run and the
row-level sense audit, not that the seed counts are unknown.*

## Hypotheses

**H1.** The function→essence / open→literal systematization that the persons domain shows recurs in the
description of the world. Terms the early canon (the four Nikāyas) leaves figurative, open, hyperbolic, or
measureless are concretized in the later strata — bounded, measured, named, enumerated, or reified — and
the concretization localizes to identifiable inter-stratum jumps (predominantly canon→commentary, with at
least one early→Abhidhamma case and at least one that intensifies again into the sub-commentary).

**H0 (pre-committed null).** Concretization in the world-description domain is NOT a recurring multi-jump
tendency. H0 wins if ANY of:
- **(a) single-jump.** All supporting transitions collapse to the one famous canon→commentary jump; no
  early→Abhidhamma hardening and no further sub-commentary intensification.
- **(b) counters dominate.** The counter-cases (the early canon already measures/bounds as often as it
  leaves open; the later strata refuse measure as often as they supply it) are ≥ the supporting cases.
- **(c) wholly definitional.** Every "support" is a measuring-term that sorts commentarial only *because*
  it measures (the measuring-text-sorts-late circularity), with no Buddha-voiced early-open anchor that is
  independent of that circularity.

## Method (pre-committed)

- **Harness:** the fine 6-stratum count CASE of record (1early / 2late / 3abh / 4para / 5comm / 6tika; a
  7th `7other` bucket holds the extra-canonical *anya* works and is reported, not used in the verdict).
  Counts are passage-rows, not occurrences. Run serially on one connection (the dhamma-pg concurrency
  wedge); never the live `/api`.
- **Stems:** test the technical COMPOUND that does the concretizing work, not the bare simplex. A 0 is
  robust only on a truncated root; inflection-final stems are truncated. Phrase-stems are flagged for the
  contiguity caveat (sandhi/`vā`-insertion can hide an early refrain).
- **Sense audit (mandatory, every positive 1early/2late count on a homograph-flagged term):** sampled rows
  are read; the target-sense fraction is reported; a term whose early hits are all non-target is coded
  **0-on-audit**. Named homograph guards, fixed in advance:
  - `avīci` bare = "without-interval / jam-packed" (AN 3.56) and `vīci` = "wave"; only `avīcinaraya` /
    `avīcimahāniraya` is the named hell.
  - the bare hell-names `saṅghāta` (= aggregate/cutting) and `tāpana` (= torment/ascetic-heat) are
    homonyms; `aṭṭhamahāniraya` is the clean discriminator.
  - `anubyañjana` bare = the sense-restraint "do not grasp the details" idiom; only `asīti-anubyañjana`
    is the 80-marks set.
  - `avyākata` bare = the Abhidhamma ethical class "karmically-indeterminate" (the dominant mass); only
    `antavā loko` / `anantavā loko` is the finite/infinite-world refusal.
  - `jambudīpa` early is mostly geographic India, not the cosmological continent.
  - `dhātu`, `appamāṇa`, `ananta`, `kappa`, `asaṅkheyya` are domain-general; restrict to the cosmological
    compound/co-occurrence.
- **Every citation resolves** to a real corpus row.

## Falsifiable predictions (scored verbatim after the run)

- **P1.** For the bounding/measuring/naming technical COMPOUNDS (items 1, 8-phrase, 10, 12, 13, 14, 15),
  the 1early count is 0 (or ≤2 and non-target on audit), with the mass in 5comm/6tika.
- **P2.** The concretization lands at ≥2 distinct jumps, not all canon→commentary: at least one
  early→Abhidhamma case (item 18, the -avacara taxonomy; and/or `antarābhav`, `gotrabhū`-style) and at
  least one that intensifies further into 6tika.
- **P3.** For the cosmic measuring vocabulary, 6tika is not uniformly lighter than 5comm — the latest
  layer keeps hardening (the persons-domain `sabhāva` 2004→4406 signature, restated for the world).
- **P4 (the H0-honest leg).** A real minority — pre-commit **≥3** of the audited transitions — run
  COUNTER: the early canon already states an exact measure (C1, MN 127), OR the later strata keep an open
  form open (C2, C3, C4, C5). If counters ≥ supports, H0(b) wins.
- **P5 (homograph guard).** The bare simplex of every homograph-flagged term over-counts the target sense
  in 1early; the technical compound is the clean discriminator. Each positive 1early count >2 on such a
  term is read on sampled rows and its target-sense fraction reported; an all-non-target early count is
  coded 0-on-audit.

## Decision rule (pre-committed)

- **H1 supported** if: ≥60% of the *audited supporting/mixed* transitions read as "support" (open→literal,
  jump-localized); the supports land at ≥2 distinct jumps; AND the counter-cases are counted but are a
  minority (P4 fails to flip).
- **H0 wins** if: counters ≥ supports (P4 flips), OR all supports collapse to the single canon→commentary
  jump (P2 fails), OR every support is wholly definitional (H0c).
- **Mixed/partial** otherwise, reported honestly with the split stated.

The expected shape, stated so it can be wrong: H1 substantially supported but not universal (mirroring the
persons domain's "strong multi-jump tendency, not a law"), with the world arm explicitly the thinner and
freshly-counted sample. If the run instead shows counters dominating or a single-jump collapse, that is a
real result and replaces the abstract's "points the same way" framing.
