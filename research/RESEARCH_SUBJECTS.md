## Notes

- The unifying methodological move across the cluster: **disambiguation up front** —
  most of these terms are polysemous, and sorting the senses is itself the finding.
- The unifying *substantive* tension: **literal vs allegorical / cosmological vs
  psychological** reading. Every cosmology study should report which layer (sutta /
  Abhidhamma / Aṭṭhakathā / Ṭīkā) each claim comes from, since that seam is where
  the debate actually lives.
