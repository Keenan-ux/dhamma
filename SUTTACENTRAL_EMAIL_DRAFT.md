# Draft email to SuttaCentral

**Subject:** Dhamma Data — using your bilara-data and parallels graph

Dear SuttaCentral team,

A short thank-you and a heads-up. I've built a small scholarly tool
at <https://dhamma.fly.dev/> that leans heavily on your published
data: Bhante Sujato's nikāya and Vinaya translations as the primary
English layer, the bilara-data Pāli text, and sc-data/parallels.json
in full (30,741 parallel, mention, and retells relationships).
In-corpus parallels render as clickable links in the reader.
Sanskrit, Chinese, and Gāndhārī parallels render as plain references
so the scholar can follow them on suttacentral.net.

The tool is non-commercial, ad-free, and telemetry-free. Each Sujato
passage carries a per-card credit and a link back to the
corresponding suttacentral.net page. It is meant as a complement for
comparative work across the Theravāda canon, commentaries,
sub-commentaries, and the ATI library, not a replacement for the
authoritative source.

If you'd prefer different attribution wording, a different surface
treatment for the parallels graph, or have any concerns about the
way your data is being re-presented, please tell me and I'll adjust.

With deep gratitude for the open-data work SuttaCentral does.

Keenan
